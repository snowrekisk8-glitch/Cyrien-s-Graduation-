Cyrien's Graduation

Written by Jacky • For Ate Cyrien

Hi ate Cyrien! HAHAHAHAHA di ko talaga at ine-expect nga magiging close kit from the start. I still remember parang naha grade 6 ata ak hat or grade 7 (BASTA BATA PAAK HIT DIDA, NGADA LA GIHAP YANA) naging fb friens kit han kan nanay fb acc, then nagchat ka hat nga may balidya ka na longganisa HAHAHAHA pag br nala ta ka nanay fb acc HAHAHAHAHA.

And now, I believe that life in a small town is a very enclosed one. Akalain mo naman fb friends la hat kita ha fb han kan nanay acc yana close na kit (hehehehe). Tbh, usa ka hat nak mga gin l-look up to han mga grade 7 ak kay shempre mga feymos ma unta kam hat HAHAHAHAHA. Gin admire ko an iyo kamangka kupal.

I know 1 year (almost) isn't enough to be with you guys, and sayang dama nga very late an at pagkakirilala. But still, yana attached na kami haiyo, nalalagas na kam tas kami na masaliwan haiyo huhuhuhu. Words aren't enough to express how thankful I am to be with you guys. Kamo nag push haak nga maging better version hitak sarili. And I will forever be thankful with you guys for that.

Just know that your college life will be more mountainous kesa han iyo gin agian yana na shs. Pero may niyo makakatagpo nga new friends (wag niyo kami ipagpalit). Mayda pirme ginsisiring haak hi mama, it sangkay kuno duduha kalain, usa magkakadto hit karat-an it usa ha kaupayan, so pili kamo hit ngaduha iton LOL. Pero bawo, tim sangkay pamili nala kamo hin tuhay kay asya it iton iyo magiging upod pirme throughout hit iyo college life. Mayda suga kada kuya Ivan, ate Marcia, Elgin, or hin-o pama it dida. Then, over all those stresses, never forget to enjoy your college life, kasi after that, sasampalon na kam hit katotohanan. You guys will have you own life, magiging busy na kam hit works or whatsoever. And, never forget nga aadi kami para haiyo, not as your juniors, but as your friends.

Never lose yourself in the process. I believe in you! PUHON!
